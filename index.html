Privacy-first by design.

CapAhead is built for personal finance, so the default is simple: your data stays on your device unless you choose to export a backup.

  • No account required to start.
    Use the app without creating a login first.
  • Device-first storage.
    Your everyday finance entries are stored locally in the browser or app storage.
  • You control backups.
    Encrypted backup tools are available when you want to move or protect your data.

Questions before you try it.

Short answers for the things people usually want to know before trusting a finance app.

Does it connect to my bank?

No. CapAhead is designed around manual entry and device-first storage, so you do not need to hand over bank-login access just to plan your week.

Where is my data stored?

Your everyday finance entries stay on your device using browser or app storage unless you choose to export a backup.

What happens if I lose my phone?

Encrypted backups and a recovery key help you restore your own data on a new device. Keep that recovery key somewhere safe.

How much does Pro cost?

Pro starts with a 7-day free trial, then A$4.99/month or A$39.99/year through Google Play. Any launch offer shown in Google Play renews at A$39.99/year after year one.
