Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

CapAhead is operated by HAC Labs. This policy explains how CapAhead handles information when you use the website or app.

Information you enter in the app

CapAhead is designed as a device-first personal finance app. Finance entries such as transactions, income sources, recurring expenses, savings, loans, and settings are stored on your device using browser or app storage.

Backups

If you choose to export a backup, you are responsible for where you save or share that file. CapAhead may provide encrypted backup tools and recovery keys so you can restore your own data later.

Website contact

If you contact us by email, we will receive the information you choose to send, such as your email address, message, and any details included in that message.

Payments and app stores

If CapAhead offers paid features, payment processing may be handled by an app store or payment provider. Those providers process payment information under their own policies.

What we do not sell

We do not sell your personal finance data.

Security

No system is perfect, but CapAhead is built to limit unnecessary collection of sensitive finance data and give you control over backups.

Contact
